Motor Vehicle cover has two options: Comprehensive or Third Party Property. Choose the car insurance that is right for you:

Comprehensive Car Insurance

Cover for your car and other people’s property
  • Cover for accidental damage to your car if you are at fault
  • Cover for accidental damage to other people’s property (including vehicles) which is partly or fully your fault
  • Damage to your car if you are not at fault in an accident and the other vehicle is uninsured
  • Accidental loss or damage to your car caused by fire or theft
  • Cover for accidental damage or loss to your car including flood, storm and hail
  • Guarantee on authorised repairs
  • Reasonable costs of towing after a covered accident

Third Party Property Damage

Cover only for other people’s property
  • Cover for accidental damage to other people’s property (including vehicles) which is partly or fully your fault
  • Approved legal costs arising out of a claim for liability covered by your policy
  • Legal liability cover when using a substitute vehicle
  • Guarantee on authorised repairs
  • Accidental damage to your car if you are not at fault in an accident and the other vehicle is uninsured. Up to a market value of $5,000
  • (OPTIONAL) Optional cover for up to $5000 for accidental loss or damage to your car caused by fire or theft.
